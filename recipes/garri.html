Soaked Garri

Description

Soaked Garri is a cold 'beverage' with ice cold water and sugar plus or minus roasted groundnuts.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup Garri
  • 3 cups water
  • Some ice cubes optional
  • 3 cubes of Sugar / 1 Tbsp
  • 2 tablespoon Powdered milk (optional)
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
  • ¼ cup roasted groundnuts (optional)
  • ¼ cup coconut

Steps

  1. Pour water into the cereal bowl containing the dry Garri
  2. Sieve out any particle that floats
  3. Pour more fresh water into the Garri
  4. Pour more fresh water into the Garri
  5. Add the groundnuts or any other topping you prefer.
